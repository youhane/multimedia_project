restoration. is a short documentary type of movie with the purpose of raising awareness regarding getting the vaccine(s) for the Coronavirus disease, with hope that it will help bring the current pandemic we face fade little by little.

The movie takes us back to when it all started and how it affected us personally and as a whole community of human civilization. From being able to do whatever we want and now being “trapped” in our own houses hearing news of our beloved ones passing one by one.

As one of the people who can join in to subside this current situation, help us and yourself by getting the vaccine.
